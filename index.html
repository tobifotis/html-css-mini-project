The Boss

Rúben Amorim is a Portuguese football manager and former player. Known for his tactical flexibility, he has gained recognition as one of Europe’s most promising young coaches. Amorim uses a 3-4-3 system and emphasizes youth development. At Sporting CP, he led the team to their first league title in years.

In 2024, Amorim became manager of Manchester United, marking a major step in his career. Tasked with rebuilding the squad, he brought a clear philosophy centered on discipline, youth, and attacking football. His vision aims to restore the club’s identity and long-term success.
